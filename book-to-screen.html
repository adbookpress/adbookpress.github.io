Hollywood Ticket

Does your book have what it takes to become the next big thing to happen in movies and television? Find out by availing the Hollywood Ticket service.

A professional reader will check and review if your book has the potential to become a movie or film adaptation. Along with this, production costs, genre, characters, and story line development are also considered for it to become an official book adaptation.

Once done, your coverage will be given to a first-look partner will determine if your book has the potential to become a book adaptation. Hollywood Director's Cut

Do not forget to add that last crucial piece for you to be considered by entertainment executives.

A Treatment is a long-form pitch for a film, television, or digital series adaptation. It is the first step to creating a draft for a screenplay which is a detailed story written for the screen. An experienced professional screenwriter will write the treatment for you, and you can give them the direction before they begin writing.

By clearly laying out how you want the approach for your book to be done, you eliminate unnecessary and mundane work for agents and producers, all while giving them a clear outline of how you want your book to be translated on screen.

After completion, your Treatment will be given to a first-look Hollywood partner who will determine if your book has what it takes to be considered for a book adaptation.

*Note: You will maintain full ownership of your story.

Hollywood Producer's Pick

Reach for your dreams and take your first steps to making your book become an adaptation through a screenplay.

Used by producers to determine if an adaptation of your book is worth every bit of their consideration, a screenplay is a fully developed script that, when professionally created, will let Hollywood executives know your serious intentions about getting into the entertainment industry.

Once your screenplay is completed, it will be available in an exclusive database that is accessible by Hollywood executives and writers. It will also be submitted to a first-look partner who will determine if your book can be considered to become an adaptation.

How does it work?

  1. A screenwriter will create a screenplay based on your approved Treatment.
  2. The screenplay will include character dialogues and details scenes and actions of the story. It will be written in industry-standard formats.
  3. Once done, it will be submitted to a first-look partner who will review the screenplay if it is fit to become an adaptation.
  4. Your screenplay will also be available in an exclusive database that is accessible by Hollywood executives and writers.
