About Us

ADBook Press is a Cebu based distribution and printing service provider of books in hardback, paperback, and e-books in numerous other formats in the Philippines.

We ensure that as a publishing services provider, manuscripts of diverse genres are accepted and the full ownership of the book is guaranteed to still belong to the author.

What We do

ADBook Press guarantees to provide quality and first-class customer service, while continuing to offer and innovate all the different services and packages that are created to ensure a smooth journey to your publishing career is delivered, and that your book is given its much-deserved leverage in the market.
