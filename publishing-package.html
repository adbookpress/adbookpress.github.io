After writing and editing your book, the job of completing the designs of your book, making it visible to major book distributors, and printing-on-demand falls to us, all while retaining all the rights to your book.

Basic

The Basic package offers all important elements and different, practical options to turn your book into a quality book. This includes enlisting your book in the online network of leading book distributors, and making it available for order in retail book stores online.

Inclusions of the Basic service are a selection of eight (8) book cover templates, five (5) book interior templates, and a distribution of cover images (supplied by the author) and an official author photo. Other features include:

Production Phase:

  • Paperback and e-book format availability of your book
  • Monitor book’s production pace and process
  • A round of book revisions & editing services (up to 25)

Post-publication Phase:

  • Five (5) Paperback copies (including a paperback Author copy)
  • Google Books preview, Registration in the Books In Print database, Amazon Look Inside, Barnes and Noble Read Instantly, Worldwide Online Book distribution
  • Assignment of an International Standard Book Number (ISBN)
  • Online book sales and royalty accounting
  • Royalty payments (quarterly)

Marketing Phase:

  • Fifty (50) pieces of postcards, business cards, and bookmarks
  • Five (5) posters
*Important note: Shipping and handling costs will be shouldered by the author.

Professional

The Professional service incorporates the services offered by the Basic package with the addition of different design options, hardback availability of the book, along with a customizable book interior template for a more one-of-a-kind feel.

Inclusions of the Professional service are a selection of eight (8) book cover templates, nine (9) book interior templates, a share of twenty-five (25) interior graphics and tables, customizable interior template elements, and a distribution of cover images (supplied by the author) and an official author photo. Other features include:

Production Phase:

  • Paperback, hardback, and e-book format availability of your book
  • Monitor book’s production pace and process
  • A round of book revisions & editing services (up to 25)

Post-publication Phase:

  • A hardback and paperback Author copy of the book
  • Ten (10) paperback copies
  • Google Books preview, Registration in the Books In Print database, Amazon Look Inside, Barnes and Noble Read Instantly, Worldwide Online Book distribution, Library of Congress registration, U.S Copyright registration
  • Assignment of an International Standard Book Number (ISBN)
  • Online book sales and royalty accounting
  • Royalty payments (quarterly)

Marketing Phase:

  • Fifty (50) pieces of postcards, business cards, and bookmarks
  • Five (5) posters
*Important note: Shipping and handling costs will be shouldered by the author.

Premium

The ideal package option for authors who do not want to compromise quality while still ensuring that they provide a fully customizable book to the market. Authors who avail of the Premium package services are given all the tools they need to design, create, and deliver a book that will ensure them a spot in the highly competitive publishing environment.

Inclusions of the Premium service are customizable book interior and cover, consultations with the designer of the book interior and cover, unlimited number of available interior graphics and tables, and a distribution of cover images (supplied by the author) and an official author photo. Other features include:

Production Phase:

  • Paperback, hardback, and e-book format availability of your book
  • Data Entry, Copy editing, and Indexing services
  • Citations-Footnotes & End notes
  • Monitor book’s production pace and process
  • Two (2) rounds of book revisions & editing services

Post-publication Phase:

  • A hardback and paperback Author copy of the book
  • Thirty (30) paperback copies, ten (10) hardback copies, and ten (10) Book stubs
  • Google Books preview, Registration in the Books In Print database, Amazon Look Inside, Barnes and Noble Read Instantly, Worldwide Online Book distribution, Library of Congress registration, U.S Copyright registration
  • Worldwide online book distribution
  • Assignment of an International Standard Book Number (ISBN)
  • CD Archive, and a set-your-own-price program
  • Bookstore returnability of the book (12 months)
  • Online book sales and royalty accounting and Royalty payments (quarterly)

Marketing phase:

  • Fifty (50) pieces of postcards, business cards, and bookmarks
  • Five (5) posters
  • Personalized Wed Design - Starter and Press Release Campaign Essential - 100 media outlets
*Important note: Shipping and handling costs will be shouldered by the author.
